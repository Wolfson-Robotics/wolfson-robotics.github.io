Wolfson Robotics
We are the WOLFPACK, First Tech Challenge, Robotics team of Samuel W. Wolfson High School. We have been actively competing in First Tech Challenge since the 2021-2022 season, and are currently one of the most successful teams in Northeast Florida, consistently ranking in the Top 5 for our region and placing high in state championships.
